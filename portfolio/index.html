A Little About Me

Welcome! My name is Carlie Fox and I'm currently in my last semester of college to recieve my degree in English.
I'm currently a writing tutor at the University of Wisconsin, but when I'm not on campus or at work, I spend my free time writing origional fiction novels and fanfiction for my blogs.
